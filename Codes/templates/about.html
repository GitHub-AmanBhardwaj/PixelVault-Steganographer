What is Image Steganography?

Image steganography is the art of concealing information within digital images. Unlike traditional encryption, which alters the visibility of data, steganography keeps the embedded information hidden, making it indistinguishable to the naked eye. This technique is perfect for secure communication, watermarking, and digital forensics.
